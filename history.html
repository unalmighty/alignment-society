Our History

From humble beginnings to a global pre‑alignment powerhouse, the Alignment Society has always been ahead of the curve—often by accident. Here’s how we got to be so misaligned we had to create alignment as a service.

2015–2017: The Alignment Awakening

Founded in a co‑working space with unlimited cold brew, the Alignment Society began as a book club dedicated to reading slide decks. By aligning on meeting agendas, we quickly realised consensus could be monetised.

2018–2020: The Synergy Boom

As businesses everywhere chased digital transformations, we pivoted to orchestrating orchestration. Our Synergy Orchestration™ offering put us on the map, even if clients still weren’t sure what we actually did.

2021–present: The Pre‑Alignment Revolution

Today, we focus on pre‑alignment: aligning on how we will align on alignment. Our proprietary frameworks ensure no misalignment goes unfunded. Join us as we align toward the next frontier.
